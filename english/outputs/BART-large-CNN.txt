Tam was a kind and beautiful girl who lost her mother at a young age.
She lived with her cruel stepmother and her half-sister, Cam.
One day during a royal festival, the king met Tam and was captivated by her beauty and grace.
He chose her to be his queen, bringing Tam a brief period of happiness.
But Cam and her mother devised a cruel plan.
They murdered Tam and deceitfully placed Cam as the new queen.
